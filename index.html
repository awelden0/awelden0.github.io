Where to Stay

The Hotel Chalet
Our wedding will take place at The Hotel Chalet, also known as the Chattanooga Choo Choo, a boutique hotel in Chattanooga, Nestled between the Tennessee River and the foothills of Appalachia. Standard single king and double queen rooms, as well as restored sleeper dexule train cars, will be available at a discounted rate.

Questions & Answers

What do I wear?

Semi-Formal: Tuxes and gowns are welcome, and so are suits and cocktail dresses. Coordinating with the color palette below is encouraged for guests, but not required.


Can I bring a plus one?

In order to maintain an intimate experience, plus ones will only be available if indicated on your invitation.

Can I bring my kids?

No, our wedding will be a child free event. As much as we love your little ones, they are not permitted to attend; enjoy a child free night with us.

Will there be food?

Yes. We will be serving a four-course, family style meal for dinner including a soup/salad, entree, side, and dessert around 6pm.

Will there be drinks?

Yes. There will be a cocktail hour immediately following the ceremony, where beer and seltzers will be available from the caboose. Following the reception dinner, enjoy an open bar on us!
